How to Make Grilled Salmon

Makes grilled salmon that is tasty and crispy with side of rice.

Salmon has a smoky flavor and is considered healthy.

Ingredients

  • 3/2 pounds of salmon fillets
  • Lemon pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1/3 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 cups of rice
  • Dipping sauce of choice
  • Handful of green onions
  • One pound of fresh asparagus spears, trimmed
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Half a lemon

Directions

  1. Put rice and water into the rice cooker and rinse the rice by pouring water and dumping it out until it is almost clear.
  2. Put middle finger on top of the rice and add water until it reaches the top of the first segment of your middle finger.
  3. Turn on rice cooker and let it cook while you prepare the salmon.
  4. Season the salmon with the lemon pepper, garlic powder, salt, and any other seasoning that you want.
  5. Mix the soy sauce, brown sugar, water, and vegetable oil into a bowl.
  6. Put the seasoned salmon and the marinade sauce into a plastic bag and let marinate in the fridge for at least 2 hours.
  7. Coat asparagus with olive oil and season with pepper and salt.
  8. Check on the rice and if done, put into another bowl.
  9. Clean the grill with a brush to prevent the skin from sticking to the grates.
  10. Preheat half of the grill to medium heat and the other half to high heat.
  11. Oil the grill grates.
  12. Grill saparagus on high heat grill for 2-3 minutes or to desired tenderness.
  13. Place the salmon on the medium heat grill skin down, close the grill, and dispose of the left-over marinade.
  14. Let the skin side grill for 6-8 minutes and then flip over, close grill, and grill for another 6-8 minutes.
  15. The salmon should be done when it flakes easily with a fork.
  16. Put the rice on a plate and put slamon on top with broccoli on side.
  17. Finish by squeezing lemon on salmon and garnishing with green onions.
